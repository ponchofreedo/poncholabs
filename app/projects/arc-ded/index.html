overview.

The ARC template really needed a face lift. It had been on my roadmap for quite awhile, and I finally got to do it towards the end of the year. It was actually probably the last big test that I ran throughout the year.

Essentially, that template was one of the last promo templates to be in my old php-based styling. It was old and crusty and needed some serious love. What I came up with was a fresh version that crushed the original version in CTR and Contribution while remaining slightly positive in Conversion...and I already have a couple of tests lined up for it in the near future. It's by no means perfect, but it was a nice change from the current version.

Old and crusty. New and updated.
