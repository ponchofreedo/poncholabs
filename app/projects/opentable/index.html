Preface...this entire test took a lot of time to implement, from the get go.

Jeff Boyd laid a challenge to us to be faster to market than Booking with an OpenTable cross-sell. The idea was going to be to lead with some fixed markets and build around those, which is what we did. Phase 1 was all hardcoded, phase 2 is all dynamic. Phase 1 design went fast...it was just the dev work that was a killer.

In phase 1.2, since we weren't seeing high click results (revealed to not be our problem) we decided to also add in the ability to download their app from the email. This just added another layer of complexity but was simple to execute.
