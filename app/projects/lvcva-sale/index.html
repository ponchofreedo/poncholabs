lvcva mutli-variant sale test

both us and the lvcva team wanted some data...so we did our first real test with them

overview.

Probably for the past like 3 years I've built at least a dozen or so different versions of the template that we send for LVCVA, and we normally just send them as is and don't test. In this particular version of the test, we wanted to see what kind of deal messaging was more compelling to the customer.

We have a lot of data that supports cheap pricing ($) to be our best weapon to increase open rate, but we also know that if the discount percentage is high enough, it can be just as good (or better). So for this particular iteration of the send, the Ad Sales team approached us and said that LVCVA was also curious about testing two separate messages: price and percent. They also wanted to introduce the same template with a sale attached to it. The primary message was the sale message, so that went to the majority of customers, but we then introduced 2 different test groups to go 50/50 price v percent.

The sale variant The percent variant The price variant

This test actually did not result in a very significant sway in either direction. We concluded that the $27 was not low enough compared to our daily $14-$19 price point AND that 62% might not have been high enough either since our normal discount is up to 60%.
