arc partner sale

a new template for airportrentalcars special partner promotions

overview.

The ARC business team approached us about introducing a new email template for them. This was spawned by requests from Avis and Budget, whom we already have special templates for, to run partner-specific promotions on a bi-weekly basis (per partner) to highlight a global discount they were offering for the remainder of the year.

I had done something similar to this in the past for the Priceline Media Group (Ad Sales) and some of their partners, but the ARC one was a bit more fun because we had 100% control over everything. Essentially, these pages just went to the partner-specific landing pages for each promo. The landing pages weren't too spectacular, so we wanted to put our best foot forward and deliver templates that were easier to digest and would hopefully drive a higher CTR and conversion on the respective landing pages.

Round 1 was a slight departure from the current deals template

This version, and the Budget version, went out as single variants the first week, and then the following week we tested them against a slightly updated version.

Round 2 was more in line with our current promo emails...specifically the backgrond container

Round 2 ended up winning by a few points in each relevant category, so we went forward and used that container with a carousel of 3 or 4 images for the hero. The plan will be to introduce more partners into the mix in the future.
