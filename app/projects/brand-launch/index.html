2016 brand launch

an email to lead-off our new brand push before the super bowl

overview.

The brand team had approached us about going in on a more expanded brand launch than we had done in years past. Normally priceline will buy a spot or 2 during a pre-game show in the morning and use it to debut a new commercial run, but this year Brett and team had tasked email, brand, and social to expand channels and get the "whatever's on the line" brand out to the masses all at once.

When it came to us, originally I was going to build an HTML5 Video template, but due to time constraints against a couple other deadlines, we opted for the direct link to the brand's YouTube playlist.

We decided to lead with "family is on the line"

What we ended up seeing, at least as reported from the social team, was that the email drove a decent amount of users into the playlist and, subsequently, the priceline keyword search got a small bump prior to the spot actually being released on tv. This was a fun one, but next time I really want to follow through on the HTML5 Video or some kind of interactive version.
